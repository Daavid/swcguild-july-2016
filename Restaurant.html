La Casa's Pizza has the best Italian Pizza. La Casa opened to serve our first pizza in June of 1953. Founder Joe Patane and family ran out of food halfway through the evening on their first night, indicating his hunch about Neopolitan-style pizza and its popularity was correct. The third generation of Joe's family invites you to share in a part of Omaha's restaurant heritage known as La Casa, still operating in our original location at 4432 Leavenworth Street.
